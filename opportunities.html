Training & Career Development

The Bar Harbor and Mount Desert Police Departments offer extensive training opportunities to our employees to prepare them for the demands of the job and to advance their careers. We pride ourselves on having highly trained Officers and Dispatchers who have the skills necessary to excel in their field and provide the highest possible level of service to our communities.

The Bar Habor and Mount Desert Police Departments strive to provide our employees with a good work-life balance. We are located adjacent to Acadia National Park which provides countless opportunities for outdoor recreation. We encourage our employees to engage with one another outside of the work environment and regularly participate in team experiences.

Our Community

Serve in one of Maine's most beautiful and vibrant communities

The communities of Bar Harbor and Mount Desert are world-renowned summer tourist destinations where millions of visitors travel each year to enjoy our beautiful sights, countless recreational opportunities, and great people. Although we enjoy a city-sized population in the summer months, we maintain our small-town vibe during the winter.

Decision Making
