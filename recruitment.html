Application Process

Our comprehensive 5-step application process ensures we select the best candidates to serve our communities. Each step is designed to evaluate your qualifications, character, and commitment to public service.

Step-by-Step Process

1

Initial Application

Submit Required Documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Resume and cover letter
  • Copy of driver's license
  • High school diploma/GED
  • College transcripts (if applicable)
  • Military DD-214 (if applicable)
2

Physical Fitness Test

Successfully complete the Maine Criminal Justice Academy Physical Fitness Test standards for your age and gender.

Test Components:

  • 1.5-mile run
  • Push-ups (1 minute)
  • Sit-ups (1 minute)
  • Sit-and-reach flexibility
View Fitness Standards
3

Interview Process

Multiple interview rounds with department leadership:

  • Panel Interview: Multiple officers and supervisors
  • Command Staff Interview: Senior leadership evaluation
  • Chief's Interview: Final interview with Police Chief
4

Background Investigation

Comprehensive background check including:

  • Criminal history review
  • Employment verification
  • Reference checks
  • Credit history review
  • Social media review
  • Neighborhood canvass
5

Medical Examination

Complete medical screening including:

  • Physical examination
  • Drug screening
  • Psychological evaluation

Pay & Benefits Package

We offer a competitive and comprehensive benefit package including a new retirement plan as of 2025 and an attractive 12-hour shift schedule to maximize time off for our employees.

Compensation

  • Starting Salary of $32.35 per hour
  • Overnight Shift Differential Pay
  • Overtime Opportunities
  • Compensatory Time Available

Work Schedule

  • 12-hour Panama Schedule for Patrol
  • Two Weeks Paid Vacation Upon Hire
  • 12 Paid Holidays
  • Up to 960 Hours of Paid Sick Time

Retirement

New for 2025

MEPERS 3C Plan

  • 25 Years of Service
  • No Age Requirement
  • 2/3's Average Final Compensation
  • Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA)

Health & Wellness

  • Paid On-Duty Exercise Time
  • $6,000 Annual Wellness Incentive
  • Comprehensive Wellness Program

Additional Benefits

  • All Uniforms and Duty Gear Provided
  • Overtime & Compensatory Time Available
  • Up to 960 Hours Paid Sick Time

Application Requirements

Applicants are not required to have a degree but must be at least 21 years old to apply.

If you are 20 years old, you must have a minimum of 60 credit hours to qualify.

Applicants are required to have already taken and passed the Maine Criminal Justice Academy Alert and Physical Fitness Test.

Visit the Maine Criminal Justice Academy website for more information
