Coffee with a Cop: Building Community at Jackson Laboratory

April 16, 2025 Community

Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department and Jackson Laboratory staff connect over coffee and conversation.

The Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department, in coordination with the Jackson Laboratory, hosted a Coffee with a Cop event this morning on the Bar Harbor Campus. These events are a great opportunity for our employees to meet people and answer lots of questions.

Officers and staff from both organizations enjoyed meaningful conversations, shared stories, and addressed a variety of questions from attendees. Events like these help foster trust, transparency, and positive relationships between law enforcement and the community we serve.

"A big thanks to Jackson Lab for having us! Please keep an eye out for other Coffee with a Cop events coming in the near future!"

The department encourages everyone to join us at future Coffee with a Cop events for a chance to connect, ask questions, and enjoy a cup of coffee together.
