Pay & Benefits

A rewarding career with competitive compensation and comprehensive support

Starting at $32.35/hour

Comprehensive Benefits Package

The Bar Harbor and Mount Desert Police Departments offer a competitive and comprehensive benefit package including a new retirement plan as of 2025 and an attractive 12-hour shift schedule to maximize time off for our employees.

Compensation

  • Starting Salary of $32.35 per hour
  • Overnight Shift Differential Pay
  • Overtime Opportunities
  • Compensatory Time Available

Work Schedule

  • 12-hour Panama Schedule for Patrol
  • Two Weeks Paid Vacation Upon Hire
  • 12 Paid Holidays
  • Up to 960 Hours of Paid Sick Time

Retirement

New for 2025

MEPERS 3C Plan

  • 25 Years of Service
  • No Age Requirement
  • 2/3's Average Final Compensation
  • Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA)

Health & Wellness

  • MMEHT PPO-1500 Health Plan
  • Paid On-Duty Exercise Time
  • $6,000 Annual Wellness Incentive
  • Comprehensive Wellness Program

Additional Benefits

  • All Uniforms and Duty Gear Provided
  • Overtime & Compensatory Time Available
  • Up to 960 Hours Paid Sick Time
