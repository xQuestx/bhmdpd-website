Application Process

Join the Bar Harbor Police Department

Year-round Hiring Competitive Salary Full Benefits

Application Steps

1

Initial Application

Submit your completed application form along with required documents:

  • Resume
  • Cover Letter
  • Copy of Driver's License
  • Copy of High School Diploma or GED
  • College Transcripts (if applicable)
  • Military DD-214 (if applicable)
2

Physical Fitness Test

Successfully complete the Maine Criminal Justice Academy Physical Fitness Test standards for your age and gender.

View Fitness Standards
3

Background Investigation

Comprehensive background check including:

  • Criminal History
  • Employment Verification
  • Reference Checks
  • Credit History
  • Social Media Review
4

Interview Process

Multiple interview rounds with:

  • Initial Panel Interview
  • Command Staff Interview
  • Chief's Interview
5

Medical Examination

Complete medical screening including:

  • Physical Examination
  • Drug Screening
  • Psychological Evaluation

Basic Requirements

Age

Must be at least 21 years old

Education

High school diploma or equivalent required; Associate's degree preferred

License

Valid Maine driver's license or ability to obtain one

Background

Clean criminal record and good moral character
