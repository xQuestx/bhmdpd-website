Become a Police Officer

Join the Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department in serving our community

Protecting Our Community Since 1896

The Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department serves the communities of Bar Harbor and Mount Desert. Our law enforcement professionals help maintain one of the lowest violent crime rates for towns of this size, protecting approximately 5,000 year-round residents and millions of seasonal visitors to Acadia National Park.

Application Process

1

Basic Requirements

  • High school diploma/GED
  • Valid driver's license
  • College credits (preferred)
  • Law enforcement/military experience (preferred)
2

Examinations

  • Pass ALERT exam (minimum score: 40)
  • Complete Physical Fitness Test
  • Submit verification with application

Maine Criminal Justice Academy: 207-877-8000

3

Interview & Background

  • Department panel interview
  • Background investigation
  • Polygraph test
4

Medical & Psychological

  • Medical examination
  • Psychological assessment
  • Drug screening
5

Academy Training

  • 18 weeks basic training
  • Law enforcement curriculum
  • Maine certification
