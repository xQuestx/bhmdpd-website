General Parking Information

Parking Guidelines

  • Handicapped vehicles park free in paid/permit areas
  • No parking on sidewalks or near hydrants
  • Maximum 72-hour street/lot parking
  • Follow posted signage for restrictions

Seasonal Permits & Information

Seasonal Hours & Dates

  • Season: May 15 - October 30
  • Monday-Saturday: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Off-Season: No paid parking required

Resident Permits

Resident Vehicle Permit

FREE

  • Residents who pay excise tax to Bar Harbor
  • One free permit per registered vehicle
  • Must provide proof of Bar Harbor registration
  • Available at Finance Department
Scan QR code on posted signs for quick application

Guest of Resident

FREE

  • Guests of residents requiring on-street parking
  • Valid in "Permit Only Parking" areas
  • Three day duration
  • Available at Parking Enforcement or Finance

Resident Recreational Boater

$24.00/day

  • For boat trailer parking in Newport Drive lot
  • One day duration
  • Available from Harbormaster or Parking Enforcement

Property Owner Permits

Non-Resident Property Owner

$75.00/season

  • Non-residents who pay property taxes to Bar Harbor
  • One permit per registered vehicle
  • Must provide proof of property ownership
  • Available at Finance Department

Bed & Breakfast

$375.00/permit

  • For B&B guests in "Permit Parking Only" areas
  • Permits issued via IPS Permit Portal
  • Available from Parking Enforcement

Home Business/Weekly Rental

$750.00/permit

  • For guests in "Permit Parking Only" areas
  • Must provide business documentation
  • Available from Parking Enforcement

Employee & Special Permits

Employee Vehicle

$30.00/season

  • For non-resident employees of Bar Harbor businesses
  • Must provide employment verification
  • Available at Finance Department

Hospital Employee

Special Fee*

  • For hospital zone (Hancock, Wayman, Center)
  • Up to 75 permit spaces
  • *$2,250.00 annually paid by hospital

College of the Atlantic Student

$30.00/season

  • For "Permit Parking Only" areas
  • Must provide student ID
  • Available at Finance Department

Special Use Permits

Commercial Fishing

$5.00/season

  • For commercial fishermen with commercial mooring
  • Maximum 30 permits per year
  • Available from Harbormaster

Contractor/Dumpster

$24.00-$54.00/day

  • For contractors with approved Building Permit
  • Limited to 5 days
  • Price varies by location (premium/non-premium)

Special Events

$24.00-$54.00/day

  • For blocking off parking spaces for events
  • One day duration
  • Price varies by location (premium/non-premium)

How to Apply

  1. Complete permit application - available online or in person
  2. Gather required documentation specific to your permit type
  3. Submit payment if applicable (plus transaction fees)
  4. Apply at appropriate department (Finance, Parking Enforcement, or Harbormaster)
Download Application Contact Parking Department

Important Information

  • Handicapped vehicles park free in all paid/permit areas
  • Permits are non-transferable between vehicles
  • Display permit clearly on dashboard
  • Transaction fees may apply to all permit applications
  • For complete documentation requirements, contact the Finance Department
