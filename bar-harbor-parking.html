Parking Kiosk Usage Guide

1

Locate Parking Kiosk

After parking, find the nearest kiosk located at central points in the parking area.

2

Payment Method

Choose your payment option:

  • Quick card insertion
  • Coins or tokens accepted
3

Enter Vehicle Plate Number

Carefully input your license plate number. Accuracy is crucial for payment verification.

4

Add Parking Time

Use the 'More Time' button to set your parking duration. Pre-payment is available, with time starting at the parking period begins.

5

Confirm Payment

Press the green 'OK' button to confirm. A ticket will be issued for your records.

6

Keep Your Ticket

Collect and retain your ticket. Your plate number is electronically sent to parking enforcement.

Additional Tips

  • Ensure kiosk screen is clearly visible
  • Double-check all entered information
  • Keep ticket until leaving parking area
