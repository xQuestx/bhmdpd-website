Parking & Transportation Guide

Parking Information

Good News: No paid parking enforcement on July 4th!

Critical Warnings

  • DO NOT park on Bar Island sand bar - vehicles risk submersion from tides
  • Newport Drive lot closes at noon - vehicles will be towed
  • Standard regulations apply - no fire hydrants or marked zones
  • DO NOT park in yellow zones - yellow zones and yellow curbs are no parking areas
  • Observe no parking signs - no parking signs are posted throughout town, please do not park in these sections

Alternative Parking

  • Athletic Field - Park Street
  • Municipal lots off Rodick Street
  • Street parking where permitted

Safety Guidelines & Regulations

Personal Fireworks Strictly Prohibited

The Town of Bar Harbor maintains a strict prohibition on all personal fireworks. This local ordinance overrides Maine state law due to high population density and fire risks.

Warning: Bringing or setting off personal fireworks is illegal and will result in fines and/or arrest

Marine Safety & Harbor Regulations

Special marine safety measures are in effect for the fireworks display:

  • 12:00 PM: The pier is closed to all boat traffic
  • Safety Zone: 200-yard radius around the end of the pier closed to all boat traffic
  • Inner Harbor Mooring Owners: Boat captains can be escorted to the skiff float for fireworks viewing
  • Post-Fireworks: Harbormaster will announce via Channel 16 when the safety zone is lifted and boat traffic can resume
