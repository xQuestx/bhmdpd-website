Seasonal Parking Regulations

Mount Desert's parking rules change dramatically between summer and winter seasons. Understanding these seasonal differences is crucial for avoiding tickets and towing.

Summer Season

May 15 - October 30

Active Enforcement Period

  • Time Limits: 30 min, 1 hr, 2 hr zones enforced
  • Paid Parking: $10/night in Northeast Harbor lots
  • Overnight Street: Generally NO - 24hr abandonment rule
  • Harbor Drive: No overnight parking (year-round)
  • Main St (Seal Harbor): No overnight (summer only)
  • Boat Trailers: Permit required in lots
  • Permits: Required in designated areas

Summer Enforcement Areas

Northeast Harbor Most restrictive - paid overnight available
Seal Harbor Limited parking with time restrictions
Somesville 2-hour limit at library
Pretty Marsh Minimal restrictions except boat ramp

Winter Season

October 31 - May 14

⚠️ CRITICAL WINTER BAN

NO PARKING on ANY Town roads
Midnight - 6:00 AM • Snow Removal
  • Purpose: Snow removal operations
  • Towing: Immediate removal at owner expense
  • Time Limits: Most suspended
  • Municipal Lots: Check individual rules
  • General Rules: Still enforced
  • Fines: Same as summer ($30)

Winter Parking Tips

Residents: Use off-street parking
Visitors: Check with accommodations
Signs: May indicate exceptions
Questions: Call (207) 276-5521

General Parking Rules

These parking rules apply year-round throughout Mount Desert. Always check posted signs as they supersede general regulations.

Where You CANNOT Park

Safety Zones

• Intersections or crosswalks
• Sidewalks or terrace areas
• Within 20 feet of fire station driveways
• Blocking fire lanes or hydrants

Traffic Areas

• Blocking traffic flow
• Blocking driveways
• On bridges
• In loading zones

Private Property

• Without owner's permission
• Business lots after hours
• Reserved spaces
• Employee parking areas

Time-Based Restrictions

30 Minutes to 2 Hours Various zones - check signs
24-Hour Abandonment Unmoved vehicles may be towed

HOW to Park Correctly

Parking Requirements

  • Parallel: Within 1 foot of curb
  • Angle: Only where marked
  • Large Vehicles: No angle parking if over 20ft
  • Direction: Park with traffic flow
  • Secure: Set brake, lock vehicle
  • Accessibility: Never block ramps

Special Vehicle Rules

Boat Trailers: Harbormaster permit required
RVs/Campers: No overnight without permit
Oversized: Check clearances & restrictions
Motorcycles: Use designated spaces
Remember: Posted signs ALWAYS supersede general rules. When in doubt, find another spot!

Fines & Enforcement

Mount Desert parking violations are civil offenses with fines ranging from $30 to $250. Save money by paying early and avoid additional fees by paying on time.

Violation Fines

$30

General Parking Violation

Early payment (14 days): $25
Standard fine: $30
Late fee (30+ days): Additional $20

$250

Handicapped Violation

Parking in handicapped space without valid placard
No early payment discount
Most serious parking offense

$50

Fire Safety Violation

Blocking fire hydrant or fire lane
Creates immediate safety hazard
May result in immediate towing

Enforcement Process

Ticket Issued Civil offense - not criminal
14 Days Early payment period ($5 discount)
30 Days Standard payment deadline
After 30 Days $20 late fee added

Permits & Paid Parking

Applications for permits will be done entirely online. The town has contracted with IPS Group to manage the process. Permit applications will be obtained, completed and managed through the IPS Group portal. Unless otherwise stated, all permits are valid for one year.

Online Applications Only: IPS Group portal • Permit approvals during normal business hours: Mon-Fri 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM • Not processed on holidays and weekends

Available Permits (All FREE)

A

Resident Vehicle Permit

For: Residents of Northeast Harbor or Seal Harbor Yacht Club where parking is not available at residence
Fee: FREE
Limit: One permit per eligible resident
Valid: Only in the village where you reside
Requirements: Current proof of residency & current vehicle registration
Note: May include verification meeting at residence
Apply: Apply for permit online
Policy: Download parking policy (PDF)

B

Boat Owners Lot Permit

For: Vessel owners with seasonal slip at Northeast Harbor Marina
Fee: FREE
Limit: 2 permits (seasonal slip) / 1 permit (transient slip)
Requirements: Current boat registration, license plate number/type & Northeast Harbor Marina receipt
Approved by: Mount Desert Harbormaster during normal business hours

C

Tennis Court Lot Permit

For: Owners or renters of moorings at Northeast Harbor Marina
Fee: FREE
Use: One overnight parking permit for Tennis Court Lot
Requirements: Current boat registration, license plate number/type & receipt from Mount Desert Harbormaster for mooring fees
Approved by: Mount Desert Harbormaster during normal business hours

D

Seal Harbor Mooring Lot Permit

For: Owners or renters of moorings in Seal Harbor
Fee: FREE
Use: One permit for overnight parking in Seal Harbor Mooring Lot (Main Street)
Requirements: Current boat registration, license plate number/type & receipt from Mount Desert Harbormaster for mooring fees
Approved by: Mount Desert Harbormaster during normal business hours
