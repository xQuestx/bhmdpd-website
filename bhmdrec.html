Application Requirements

Applicants are not required to have a degree but must be at least 21 years old to apply.

If you are 20 years old, you must have a minimum of 60 credit hours to qualify.

Applicants are required to have already taken and passed the Maine Criminal Justice Academy Alert and Physical Fitness Test.

Visit the Maine Criminal Justice Academy website for more information
