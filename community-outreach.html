Community Outreach

Connecting our community with essential resources and support services

Supporting Our Community

The Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department is committed to serving our community beyond traditional law enforcement. We believe in connecting residents with the resources they need to thrive. This page provides information about various community resources available to residents of Bar Harbor, Mount Desert, and surrounding areas.

If you need assistance finding the right resource or have questions about any of the services listed below, please contact us and we'll be happy to help.
