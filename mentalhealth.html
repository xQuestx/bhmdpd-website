Bar Harbor & Mount Desert Police Mental Health Unit

Serving Our Island Communities with Compassion and Understanding

Mental Health Unit Overview

The Bar Harbor-Mount Desert Police Department's Mental Health Unit represents our commitment to providing specialized support for Mount Desert Island community members experiencing mental health crises.

Our Philosophy

Mental health calls represent some of law enforcement's most complex challenges, particularly in our island community where resources can be limited. Our dedicated unit approaches each situation with compassion, understanding that connection to appropriate mental health services, not criminal charges, often provides the best path forward.

Emergency Response

When to Call 911

Call 911 Immediately If:
  • Someone has attempted or is at risk of self-harm
  • A person in mental health crisis poses a danger to others
  • Immediate medical attention is needed
  • The situation requires immediate police response

What to Expect

When you call 911 for a mental health emergency, our specially trained officers will respond with compassion and understanding. Our Mental Health Unit works to de-escalate situations and connect individuals with appropriate care.
