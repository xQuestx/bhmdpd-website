Why Join Our Team?

Streamlined Application

Our 5-step application process is designed to find the best candidates while respecting your time. From application to job offer, we guide you through each stage.

Excellent Benefits

Competitive starting salary of $32.35/hour, comprehensive health coverage, retirement plan, and extensive paid time off. We invest in our officers' well-being.

Career Growth

Numerous specialties available including Marine Patrol, K-9 Unit, Detective Division, and Community Outreach. Shape your career path with ongoing training.

Professional Training

Extensive job-specific training at the Maine Criminal Justice Academy and ongoing professional development. We're committed to your success.

Basic Requirements

No degree required! We value character, dedication, and a commitment to serve.

Age Requirements

  • Must be at least 21 years old to apply
  • OR be 20 years old with a minimum of 60 college credit hours

Education

  • High school diploma or GED required
  • Associate's degree preferred but not required
  • Continuing education opportunities available

Licensing

  • Valid Maine driver's license or ability to obtain
  • Clean driving record
  • Maine law enforcement certification or eligibility

Background

  • Clean criminal record
  • Good moral character
  • Strong community standing

Physical Fitness

  • Pass Maine Criminal Justice Academy fitness test
  • Meet age and gender-specific standards
  • Maintain fitness throughout career

Skills & Abilities

  • Strong communication skills
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Emotional intelligence and empathy

Important Information

Maine Criminal Justice Academy Testing:

All applicants must have already taken and passed the Maine Criminal Justice Academy ALERT test and Physical Fitness Test before applying. Visit the MCJA website for testing dates and locations.

Lateral Transfers Welcome:

Currently certified Maine law enforcement officers are encouraged to apply. We offer competitive incentives for experienced officers looking to join our team.
