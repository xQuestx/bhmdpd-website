Application Process

Our comprehensive 5-step application process ensures we select the best candidates to serve our communities.

Application Steps

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit your application with all required documents including resume, cover letter, and educational transcripts.

2

Physical Fitness Test

Pass the Maine Criminal Justice Academy fitness standards including 1.5-mile run, push-ups, sit-ups, and flexibility test.

3

Background Investigation

Comprehensive background check including criminal history, employment verification, and reference checks.

4

Interview Process

Multiple interview rounds including panel interview, command staff interview, and final interview with the Chief.

5

Medical & Psychological

Complete physical examination, drug screening, and psychological evaluation to ensure fitness for duty.

Important Information

MCJA Testing Required: All applicants must have already taken and passed the Maine Criminal Justice Academy ALERT test and Physical Fitness Test before applying.

Lateral Transfers Welcome: Currently certified Maine law enforcement officers are encouraged to apply. We offer competitive incentives for experienced officers.

Maine Criminal Justice Academy

New officers attend the 18-week Basic Law Enforcement Training Program at the Maine Criminal Justice Academy.

Academy Training

The Basic Law Enforcement Training Program (BLETP) is an intensive 18-week residential program that prepares you for a career in law enforcement.

Maine Criminal Justice Academy training prepares officers for real-world situations

Training Includes:

  • Criminal Law & Procedures
  • Emergency Vehicle Operations
  • Defensive Tactics
  • Firearms Training
  • Crisis Intervention
  • Community Policing
  • First Aid & CPR
  • Investigations
