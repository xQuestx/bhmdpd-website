Patrol Division

Protecting and serving the Bar Harbor and Mount Desert communities

About Our Division

The Patrol Division is the backbone of the Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department, providing 24/7 law enforcement services to our communities. Our officers are dedicated to maintaining public safety, responding to emergencies, and building positive relationships with community members.

Our Responsibilities

  • Emergency response
  • Crime prevention and investigation
  • Traffic enforcement
  • Community policing
  • Public assistance

Services We Provide

Traffic Safety

Our officers enforce traffic laws to ensure the safety of drivers, pedestrians, and cyclists throughout Bar Harbor and Mount Desert.

Criminal Investigations

We investigate criminal activity to bring justice to victims and maintain the safety of our community.

Community Engagement

Building relationships with community members through outreach programs, school visits, and public events.

Emergency Response

Rapid response to emergencies and critical incidents to protect life and property.
