About Our Patrol Division

The Patrol Division is the backbone of the Bar Harbor Mount Desert Police Department, providing continuous law enforcement services to our communities. Our officers are the first responders, dedicated to maintaining public safety, enforcing laws, and building positive relationships with residents and visitors alike.

Core Responsibilities

Our officers are trained and equipped to handle a wide range of responsibilities to ensure community safety and well-being.

Emergency Response

Rapidly responding to 911 calls, including crimes in progress, medical emergencies, and accidents.

Proactive Patrol

Maintaining a visible presence in neighborhoods and business districts to deter crime.

Traffic Enforcement

Ensuring road safety through enforcement of traffic laws and accident investigation.

Community Policing

Engaging with the public to build trust and collaboratively solve local issues.

Comprehensive Law Enforcement Services

Our Patrol Division provides a wide array of services to ensure the safety and security of all residents and visitors in Bar Harbor and Mount Desert.

Traffic Safety

Proactive enforcement and education to ensure safe roadways for drivers, pedestrians, and cyclists.

  • Speed & OUI Enforcement
  • Accident Investigation
  • School Zone Safety

Criminal Investigations

Thorough investigation of criminal activity to bring justice to victims and maintain community safety.

  • Crime Scene Processing
  • Evidence Collection
  • Witness Interviews

Emergency Response

Rapid, 24/7 response to critical incidents, working with fire and EMS to protect life and property.

  • 911 Emergency Calls
  • Crisis Intervention
  • Medical Assists

Property Protection

Residential and commercial property checks, alarm response, and burglary prevention to keep communities secure.

  • Property Checks
  • Alarm Response
  • Vacation Watches
