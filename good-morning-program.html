Good Morning Program

Daily wellness checks for our senior community members

How It Works

Daily Check-ins

Monday through Friday
8:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Daily Wellness Calls
Safety Follow-ups
Community Care
Support Network

Who Can Participate?

  • Senior citizens living alone in Mount Desert and Bar Harbor
  • Adults with disabilities residing independently
  • Bar Harbor and Mount Desert residents

Program Process

Step 1: Daily Call

Our team makes friendly check-in calls each morning

Step 2: Verification

Well-being is confirmed through phone contact

Step 3: Follow-up

If needed, wellness checks are conducted in person

Download and complete the enrollment form to get started with this free, voluntary program.
