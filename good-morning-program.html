Caring for Our Community

The Good Morning Program is a free service providing daily wellness checks for senior citizens and adults with disabilities who live alone in Bar Harbor and Mount Desert. Our dedicated team ensures the safety and well-being of our community's most vulnerable residents.

Daily Phone Calls

Friendly check-in calls every weekday morning to ensure participants are safe and well.

Immediate Response

If we can't reach you, officers are dispatched to check on your well-being in person.

Community Connection

Building relationships between residents and the police department through daily interaction.

Program Process

Step 1: Morning Call

Our team calls you between 8:30 AM and 10:30 AM, Monday through Friday.

Step 2: Check-In

A brief, friendly conversation to confirm you're doing well and don't need assistance.

Step 3: Follow-Up

If we can't reach you after multiple attempts, an officer visits your home.

Step 4: Emergency Response

If needed, we coordinate with emergency services to ensure your safety.

Flexibility: Going away? Just let us know, and we'll pause your calls until you return.

Who Can Participate?

Eligible Participants

  • Senior citizens (65+) living alone
  • Adults with disabilities living independently
  • Residents of Bar Harbor or Mount Desert
  • Those who would benefit from daily check-ins

Service Areas

Bar Harbor

All residential areas within Bar Harbor town limits

Mount Desert

All residential areas within Mount Desert town limits

Note: This program is specifically for individuals living alone who may need daily wellness checks.
